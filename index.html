We do one thing well — proper BBQ, cooked low and slow, served from a counter at your event. No silver cloches. No three-course formality. Just great food, fast service, and guests who actually enjoy their meal.

The food

Slow-cooked.
Worth the wait.

Everything is prepared with time — not shortcuts. The results speak for themselves.

Signature

Texas Smoked Beef Brisket

Rubbed with salt, pepper, and patience. Smoked for 12+ hours until the bark is set and the meat pulls clean. Rich, smoky, and properly beefy.

Fan favourite

Burnt Ends

Brisket point cubed, resauced, and smoked a second time until lacquered and tender. Caramelised on the outside, falling apart in the middle. Worth every minute.

House made

Smokehouse Sausage

Coarse ground, lightly seasoned, smoked through. Sliced and served alongside the mains or as a standalone option. Simple and honest.

All mains served with slaw, potato salad, and pickles.
