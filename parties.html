How it works

Same food.
Any occasion.

The setup is simple. We bring the food, we run the counter, your guests eat well. Whether it's 40 people in a garden or 200 in a warehouse, the approach is the same.

We handle all the food logistics

Prep, transport, setup, service, and clear-down. You focus on the party.

Counter service keeps it social

Guests queue, fill their trays, and eat when they like. No waiting for a table to be called.

Minimum 40 guests

We work best at scale — from 40 up to around 300. Drop us a message if you're not sure.
